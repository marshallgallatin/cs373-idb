Jambalaya

Ingredients

Instructions

Brown sausage. Blacken fish with pepper and paprika. Brown in hot skillet.
Saute onion, celery, green pepper, garlic until limp.
Add stock and tomato sauce, spices, vegetable and meats to crock pot or large stock pot
Boil for 1 hour on low or crock pot until done.
